About me

I was first introduced to programming in highschool by a friend who taught himself python. At first I was intimidating by the complexity and thought it was a niche field to study. As I started to learn more about technology, more specifically web development, I realized how broad and significant the field is. I switched from a pre-med student to computer science and started working on solo projects.

Outside of web development, I volunteer a lot of my time at Savannah's BAPS Swaminarayan Temple. I am part of a team that plans and carry out events and activities around the temple. I am also an avid sports fan, with the misfortune of being a Dallas Cowboys fan.
